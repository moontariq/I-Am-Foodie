Homemade French Fries

Homemade French fries are a beloved classic, offering a delightful combination of crispy exteriors and tender interiors. Made from fresh russet potatoes, these fries are meticulously cut into thin strips, soaked to remove excess starch, and then fried to golden perfection in hot vegetable oil. Seasoned with a sprinkle of salt and optional herbs or spices, each fry delivers a satisfying crunch followed by a melt-in-your-mouth experience. Served hot, they are perfect for dipping in ketchup, mayonnaise, or your favorite sauce, making them an irresistible snack or side dish that complements any meal. Whether garnished with parsley for an extra touch of flavor or enjoyed plain, homemade French fries are a timeless treat that brings comfort and joy to every bite.
