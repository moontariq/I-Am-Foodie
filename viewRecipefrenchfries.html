Ingredients

  • Potatoes: 4-5 medium-sized russet potatoes
  • Vegetable Oil: For frying about 2-3 cups
  • Salt: To taste
  • Optional Seasonings: Paprika, garlic powder, black pepper, parsley for garnishing.
  • Dipping Sauces: Ketchup, mayonnaise, or any preferred dip

    • Instruction

  • Wash and peel the potatoes.
  • Cut them into thin strips, about 1/4 inch thick.
  • Soak the cut potatoes in a bowl of cold water for at least 30 minutes (this helps to remove excess starch and makes the fries crispier).
  • Heat the oil in a large frying pan or deep fryer to 350°F (175°C).
  • Drain the potatoes and pat them dry with a paper towel.
  • Fry the potatoes in batches to avoid overcrowding the pan. Fry for about 3-4 minutes or until they are golden and crispy.
  • Use a slotted spoon to remove the fries from the oil and place them on a paper towel-lined plate to drain excess oil.
  • Immediately season the fries with salt and any optional seasonings while they are still hot.
  • Garnish with chopped parsley if desired.
  • Serve with your favorite dipping sauces.
